# Nestaro Lead Qualifier — Features & Value

An AI agent that answers a local service business's Facebook Messenger inbox, qualifies every lead, books the appointment, and hands a finished, contact-ready lead to the owner — automatically, 24/7.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Local service businesses (duct & dryer-vent cleaning, home services) lose money on the leads they already paid for:

- **Slow replies kill deals.** Customers message a Page and expect an answer in minutes. Owners are on a job, asleep, or overwhelmed — so leads go cold and book with whoever answered first.
- **Every inquiry is a manual grind.** The same questions every time: what service, what area, how many units, what's the price, name, phone, address, what time works. It eats the owner's day.
- **No qualification means wasted trips.** Without checking the service area and job details up front, owners drive to addresses they don't cover or quote the wrong price.
- **Leads leak and get forgotten.** Conversations live scattered in the Page inbox with no record, no follow-up, no single place to see who's booked.
- **Out-of-area demand is invisible.** Requests from places the business doesn't serve yet just vanish — the owner never learns where demand is building.

**The result:** paid ad clicks and organic inquiries that never turn into booked jobs.

Nestaro answers instantly, in a human voice, qualifies the lead properly, books a real open slot, and notifies the owner — so no lead is dropped and no owner time is spent until there's a confirmed job on the calendar.

---

## What It Does (Features)

### Conversational Lead Qualification
- Replies to incoming Messenger messages **automatically, around the clock**, as a **named company representative** (not an obvious bot).
- Runs a **structured qualification flow**: greet → identify the service → check the service area → confirm quantity and quote the price → collect name, phone, and address → book a time.
- Asks **one question at a time**, remembers what's already been said, and never re-asks for information the customer already gave.
- Handles **non-text messages** (photos, stickers, voice notes) by politely steering back to text.

### Service-Area Gating
- **Confirms the customer's area before quoting or booking**, so the business never commits to jobs outside its coverage.
- **Politely declines out-of-area customers** without wasting their time — and **silently alerts the owner** about that missed demand, so they can see where customers are asking from.

### Accurate Pricing
- Quotes **only the real prices** the business has configured (per-unit pricing, combo deals) — the agent never invents a number.
- **Escalates discount or special-case requests** to a human instead of guessing.

### Real Appointment Booking
- Offers the customer **genuinely open time slots** based on business hours, existing bookings, job duration, buffer/travel time between jobs, and how many crews can work in parallel.
- **No same-day and no closed-day bookings** — only real, valid times.
- **Conflict-safe:** if a slot gets taken at the last second, the agent apologizes and offers the next open times instead of double-booking.
- Accepts customer-proposed times and validates them against the real schedule.
- Lets owners **block off personal time** so those windows are never offered.

### Returning-Customer Recognition
- Recognizes people who've messaged before, **greets them by name**, and skips questions it already has answers to.
- Knows their existing appointment and past details, so repeat contact feels personal, not repetitive.

### Human Escalation & Takeover
- When the agent hits something it shouldn't answer, it **stalls gracefully** ("Let me check on that for you") and **alerts the owner**, who can reply once and let the agent resume.
- The owner can **take full control** of any conversation at any time — the agent goes silent — and **hand control back** when done.

### Instant Owner Notifications
- The moment a lead is fully qualified and booked, the owner gets an **email with the complete lead**: name, phone, address, service, price, and appointment time.
- Separate alerts for **escalations** and **out-of-area inquiries**.

### Owner Dashboard
- A secure, login-protected dashboard to **view all businesses, leads, and full conversation transcripts** in one place.
- **Calendar view** of upcoming appointments.
- **Stale-conversation alerts** so nothing sits unanswered, plus manual controls to reset a conversation's state.

### Multi-Business Ready
- Runs **many businesses on one system**, each with its own Facebook Page, persona, pricing, hours, and leads — fully **isolated** from one another.
- Each business's **agent personality, business details, and conversation style are fully customizable** without touching anything technical.
- A **guided setup** onboards a new business end to end — persona, services, pricing, hours, and Page connection.

### Reliable by Design
- **Human-paced replies** so conversations feel natural, not robotic.
- **Never double-processes** a message and **never drops** an inbound message, even under errors.
- Built to keep responding quickly even during quiet periods.

## Who It's For

- **Local service businesses** (duct/dryer-vent cleaning and similar home services) that get leads through Facebook Messenger and can't answer them fast enough.
- **Owners and small teams** who want every inquiry qualified and booked without living in their inbox.
- **Operators running multiple businesses or Pages** who need one place to manage all of it.

---

## The Bottom Line

Nestaro turns a Facebook Page inbox into an always-on booking engine: **every lead gets an instant, human reply, gets properly qualified, and lands on the calendar as a confirmed appointment** — while the owner only steps in when it actually matters.
